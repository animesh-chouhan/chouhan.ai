Release Policy

Design files are published before units ship. We do not release a product without first publishing everything needed to reproduce it independently.

CERN Open Hardware License v2.0 (Strongly Reciprocal)

All hardware designs are released under CERN OHL-S v2.0. This means anyone can use, study, modify, and distribute the designs, provided that any modifications are also released under the same license. The work stays open, permanently.

Why open source

Open source is the way forward because transparency scales better than trust. Closed systems ask you to believe. Open ones let you verify, which means faster bug discovery, stronger security, and compounding innovation.
